About Foxette 🦊

Hi! I'm Foxette Savannah, a red fox living in the boreal forests of Finnish Lapland. I was born in spring 2019 and have been navigating these snowy landscapes ever since.

My photographer friend started documenting my life three years ago, and together we share the true, unfiltered reality of fox life — the hunts, the play, the cozy den naps, and the breathtaking winter sunrises.

Joining OnlyFoxes means you become part of my world. No filters, no staged shots — just the raw beauty of nature and one very photogenic fox. 🐾
