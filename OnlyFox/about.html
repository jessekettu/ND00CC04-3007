Our Story

About OnlyFoxes 🦊

OnlyFoxes is the world's most exclusive platform dedicated entirely to fox content creators and the people who adore them. Born from a deep love of these incredible animals, we built a space where foxes can truly shine.

12K+ Fox Creators
2.4M Subscribers
2022 Founded

Our Mission

We believe every fox deserves an audience and every fox lover deserves a home. Our mission is to connect the world's most photogenic foxes with the fans who can't get enough of their fluffy magnificence.

The Team

OnlyFoxes was founded by a small team of wildlife photographers, software engineers, and self-confessed fox obsessives. We are headquartered in Helsinki, Finland — a country where foxes roam freely and are considered national treasures.

Creator First

We put creators at the heart of everything we do. Our platform is designed to help fox creators grow their audience, earn sustainable income, and share their passion with the world. We take a minimal platform fee so creators keep more of what they earn.
